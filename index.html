Eligibility & Registration

Eligibility

  • Open to Engineering Students (UG).
  • Teams from the same or different departments of the same college are allowed.
  • Each team must have 4 to 5 members with at least one female member.
  • Participants must carry their own laptops.
  • Each team can register for only one problem statement.

Registration

  • Registration is FREE (Maximum of two teams per college are allowed).
  • Registration Deadline: 15 October 2025.
